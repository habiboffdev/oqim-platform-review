Data Deletion

Last updated: 12 June 2026

You can request deletion of the personal data OQIM holds about you, including data we received from Instagram or Telegram. This page explains how.

If you are a business using OQIM

  • Disconnect a channel. Disconnecting an Instagram or Telegram account in your OQIM workspace revokes the stored access token so it is no longer used.
  • Delete your account and data. To delete your OQIM account and the data associated with it, email us at the address below and we will process the request.

If you are a customer who messaged a business

If you sent messages to a business through OQIM (for example, an Instagram DM or comment), you can ask us to delete the data we hold about those conversations. Because that data belongs to the business you contacted, we will act on it as the business’s processor.

How to request deletion

  1. Email privacy@your-domain.example with the subject line Data Deletion.
  2. Tell us who you are: for a business, the workspace or account name; for a customer, the Instagram or Telegram username you used and the business you contacted.
  3. We may ask one or two questions to verify the request and prevent someone deleting another person’s data.

We confirm and complete verified deletion requests within 30 days. We may retain limited records where the law requires it or to resolve disputes; we will tell you if that applies.

Contact

Data deletion and privacy requests: privacy@your-domain.example. See also our Privacy Policy.
