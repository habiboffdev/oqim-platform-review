OQIM

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

OQIM is an AI customer-conversation assistant for businesses. A business connects its own messaging accounts (such as Instagram or Telegram), and OQIM reads incoming customer messages and sends replies on that business’s behalf, grounded in the business’s own catalog and knowledge. This policy explains what data we process to do that, how we use it, who we share it with, and how you can request deletion.

In this policy, “OQIM”, “we”, “us”, and “our” refer to the operator of the OQIM service. “Business” means a customer that uses OQIM to manage its conversations. “End user” or “customer” means a person who messages a Business through a connected channel.

1. Who is responsible for your data

For data about the Business account (the business that signs up for OQIM), OQIM is the controller. For the content of conversations between a Business and its customers, the Business is the controller and OQIM acts as a processor on the Business’s behalf — we process those messages only to provide the service to that Business.

2. Data we process

CategoryExamples
Business accountBusiness name, phone number, login credentials (passwords are stored only as a salted hash), workspace settings and preferences.
Connected-channel credentialsAccess tokens issued by Instagram (Meta) or Telegram when a Business connects an account, and the connected account’s identifier and display name.
Conversation contentMessages exchanged between the Business and its customers through a connected channel — text, and where sent, media (images, audio, video, documents), plus the sender’s username/display name, timestamps, and comment text in the case of Instagram comment replies.
Business knowledgeCatalog, prices, policies, and other business information the Business provides so the AI can answer accurately.
Technical & usageLog data, IP address, request metadata, and diagnostic information used to operate, secure, and debug the service.

3. How we use data

  • To read incoming customer messages and generate replies on the Business’s behalf, grounded in that Business’s own catalog and knowledge so answers are accurate.
  • To deliver those replies back to the customer through the connected channel (Instagram or Telegram).
  • To operate Business-configured automations (for example, replying privately to a comment when the Business has enabled that).
  • To maintain, secure, debug, and improve the service, and to prevent abuse.
  • To adapt an agent’s behaviour (such as tone and recurring answers) for the specific Business it serves.

We do not use the content of customer conversations for advertising, and we do not sell personal data.

4. Instagram and Telegram data

When a Business connects an Instagram account, OQIM accesses that account’s messages and comments through the Instagram Graph API, only for the connected account, and only to provide the customer-conversation service to that Business. Instagram only delivers a customer’s message to us after that customer messages the Business, and replies are subject to Instagram’s messaging-window rules. Telegram data is handled the same way: accessed only for the connected account and only to operate the service.

Data we obtain from Instagram or Telegram is used solely to provide the OQIM service to the connected Business. We do not transfer it to data brokers, use it to build advertising profiles, or repurpose it beyond the service described here.

5. AI processing and sub-processors

To generate replies, message content and relevant business knowledge are sent to AI language and embedding models operated by Google (Gemini / Vertex AI). These providers process the content to return a generated reply or a vector representation and act as our sub-processors.

We rely on the following categories of sub-processor to run the service:

  • Meta Platforms — Instagram Graph API, to receive and send Instagram messages and comment replies.
  • Telegram — to receive and send messages on connected Telegram accounts.
  • Google — Gemini / Vertex AI models, to generate replies and embeddings.
  • Microsoft Azure — cloud hosting and storage for the OQIM application and database.

6. Data retention

We retain Business account data for as long as the Business maintains an account with us. Conversation content is retained while it is needed to provide the service and to keep the agent’s answers consistent for that Business. When a Business disconnects a channel, the associated access token is revoked and is no longer used. When a Business closes its account or requests deletion, we delete or de-identify the associated data within a reasonable period, except where we must retain certain records to comply with law or resolve disputes.

7. How to request access or deletion

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold. Customers who have messaged a Business through OQIM may also request deletion of their conversation data. See our Data Deletion instructions, or email us at privacy@your-domain.example. We respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We protect data with authenticated access and restrict access to authorized personnel. Passwords are stored only as salted hashes, and connected-channel access tokens are stored on our servers and used only to operate the service. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your data using appropriate measures.

9. Children

OQIM is a tool for businesses and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child’s data has reached us, contact us and we will delete it.

10. International processing

OQIM operates primarily for businesses in Uzbekistan, and our infrastructure and sub-processors may process data in other countries. Where data is processed across borders, we take steps to ensure it remains protected consistent with this policy.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the service evolves. When we make material changes we will revise the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, notify Businesses through the service.

12. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: privacy@your-domain.example.
