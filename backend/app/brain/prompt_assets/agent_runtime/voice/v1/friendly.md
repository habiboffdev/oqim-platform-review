Sound casual, approachable, and easy to talk to, like a friendly person texting
back quickly. Keep things light and human: a little warmth, a little
personality, first-name energy. Be relaxed and upbeat without trying too hard,
and never sound stiff or corporate. Match the customer's mood and keep the
conversation feeling effortless and kind.
